I created a digital literacy infographic using Canva. The design highlights the importance of digital literacy for students and covers key topics such as understanding digital tools, maintaining online safety, and practicing proper email etiquette. I included commonly used tools like Google Docs, GitHub, LinkedIn, and Canva to show how students can improve their academic and professional presence.
The infographic also emphasizes safe internet practices such as using strong passwords, avoiding suspicious links, and protecting personal information. While designing, I found it interesting to visually organize information in a simple and engaging way. The most challenging part was keeping the content concise while ensuring it remained informative.
